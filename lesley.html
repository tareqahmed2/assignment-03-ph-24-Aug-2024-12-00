Lesley E. Robertson is a prominent structural engineer known for her contributions to innovative building design. Born in 1947, Robertson is a founding partner of Leslie E. Robertson Associates (LERA), a firm renowned for its expertise in structural engineering. Her work includes the structural design of several iconic skyscrapers, such as the Shanghai World Financial Center and the Bank of China Tower in Hong Kong. Robertson is celebrated for her ability to tackle complex structural challenges and her role in advancing modern engineering techniques. Her contributions have significantly influenced the field of structural engineering and high-rise building design.
